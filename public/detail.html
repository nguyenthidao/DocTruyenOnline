The Magic Meadow
Rebecca

My storybook, as you will find,
Celebrates the nosey kind.

Here we meet a nosey fellow,
He's just found a magic meadow!

He trots right in, and before long,
He's quite forgotten where he's gone!

Lost!
He walks for hours and hours,
But all he sees are stalks and flowers!

He thinks to ask a passer-by,
And says "Excuse me Dragonfly!"

"I'm busy!" buzzes Dragonfly,
"I'm catching frogs to make a pie!"

'Isn't that the wrong way round?'
He thinks what a strange place he's found!



When suddenly down at his toes,
He hears a divttle voice, which goes:

"Good morning Jones!"
"Good morning Grey!"
"Shall we see the birds today?"

"Indeed, as we have such fine weather,
Perhaps we'll have a picnic together!"

'Birds and worms the best of friends?
Well, the strangeness never ends!'

He wanders on, but all he sees,
He finds rather hard to bediveve...

He meets a dog with candy fur,
But then that dog begins to purr!

He sees a cat with two left feet...

...And cakes that no one wants to eat!

And then, under the sun's warm glow,
Somehow it begins to snow!

Puzzled by snow that isn't cold,
He plans to hide inside a hole.

But as he does, he starts to sdivde...

...and finds that he is back outside!
